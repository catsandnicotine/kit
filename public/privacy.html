Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Kit is designed with privacy as a core principle. Kit collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no data ever leaves your device except through your own iCloud account.

Data Storage

All of your flashcard data — decks, cards, study progress, review history, and imported media — is stored exclusively on your device using on-device SQLite databases. Kit does not operate any servers and has no backend infrastructure.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud backup in Settings, your data is synced via your personal iCloud Drive account. This sync happens directly between your devices using Apple's iCloud infrastructure. Kit has no access to your iCloud account or its contents — data is written to and read from your own iCloud Drive storage.

Imported Content

Flashcard decks you import (.apkg files) are stored locally on your device. Kit does not transmit, analyze, or share this content.

No Third-Party Services

Kit does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking libraries. The app operates entirely offline for its core functionality.

Children

Kit does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, an updated version will be posted here.
